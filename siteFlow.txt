Site login with Patreon:
- On Patreon button click, nav to Patreon login
- Auth then generate JWT
- Pass JWT in route to front-end
- Store in localStorage
- Client uses JWT to change settings

Twitch:
- On extension load, Twitch credentials passed down
- On click Patreon button
  - SignalR group joined (Twitch ChannelId)
  - Patreon Auth window opened
- On successful Patreon login
    - SignalR push
    - Settings loaded
- On settings change, Twitch Access Token checked
